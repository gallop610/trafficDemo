Vehicle Routing Optimizer

The Vehicle Routing Optimizer is a web application which allows logistics providers to plan and optimize the navigation routes for a fleet of vehicles needing to transport goods, services or people to multiple locations with time and capacity constraints.This is a brand new product from the Department of Industrial Systems Engineering and Management of the Department of Industrial Systems Engineering and Management of the National University of Singapore that aims to translate some of the best Optimisation and Routing research work into meaningful end products to solve traffic and logistics problems around the world.
